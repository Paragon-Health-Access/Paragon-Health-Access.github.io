1. What Is Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate marketing is a performance-based marketing model in which an independent publisher (Paragon Frontier Capital LLC DBA Paragon Health Access) earns a commission by referring customers to a merchant (a telehealth provider) through a unique tracking link. When you click one of our affiliate links and complete a qualifying action — such as enrolling in a program — we receive a commission from the provider.

This commission comes from the provider, not from you. The price you pay for any program is not affected by whether you arrived through our affiliate link or directly.

3. How Our Commissions Work

Paragon Frontier Capital LLC earns commissions only when a qualifying action is completed — typically when a visitor enrolls in a paid program through our affiliate link. Commission rates vary by provider and program. We do not earn commissions simply for displaying a provider's name or for clicks that do not result in enrollment.

Our commission arrangements do not influence which providers we feature, how we describe them, or how we rank them. We only partner with providers we believe offer legitimate, licensed, and transparently priced programs.

4. Our Editorial Standards

Despite our affiliate relationships, Paragon Frontier Capital LLC is committed to providing honest, accurate, and unbiased information. Specifically:

5. FTC Compliance

This disclosure is made in accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials, specifically 16 CFR Part 255. The FTC requires that material connections between endorsers and advertisers — including affiliate compensation — be clearly and conspicuously disclosed to consumers.

Paragon Frontier Capital LLC takes this obligation seriously. Affiliate disclosures appear:

  • On this dedicated Affiliate Disclosure page
