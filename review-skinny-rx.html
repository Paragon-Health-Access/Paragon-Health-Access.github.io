Overview

SkinnyRx offers GLP-1 weight loss programs through licensed physicians with fast online intake and direct-to-door delivery. They have built a broad nationwide operation with a streamlined patient enrollment process. In terms of commission rates, SkinnyRx is among the highest in the affiliate market at $500–$600 per new patient — reflecting the confidence they place in affiliate-driven acquisition.

We feature SkinnyRx with an important caveat: the GLP-1 compounding market they operate in has faced intense FDA scrutiny in 2025–2026. We recommend patients verify their current medication sourcing and compliance posture before enrolling. Our compliance monitoring is ongoing and this page will be updated if the situation changes materially.

Why We Still Feature SkinnyRx

Despite the compliance monitoring notice, we continue to feature SkinnyRx for two reasons:

  • No active FDA warning letter confirmed — Unlike MEDVi which received a confirmed FDA warning letter in February 2026, we have not confirmed active enforcement action against SkinnyRx directly. We monitor this actively.
  • Patient demand exists — Many patients specifically seek programs in the price range SkinnyRx operates in. Removing them entirely would leave patients without information about a program they may choose regardless. Our role is to inform, not to gatekeep.

What to Verify Before Enrolling

Given the regulatory environment, we recommend verifying these specific points directly with SkinnyRx before submitting payment:

  • What pharmacy fulfills their prescriptions and under what legal compounding pathway
  • Whether their medication is FDA-approved branded or compounded — and if compounded, the specific legal basis
  • Current pricing and what is included in the monthly fee
  • Cancellation and refund terms in writing before enrolling
  • State-specific availability confirmation
