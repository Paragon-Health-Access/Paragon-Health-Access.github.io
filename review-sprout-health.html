Overview

Sprout Health offers a streamlined GLP-1 telehealth model built around one non-negotiable standard — physician oversight on every prescription. In a market where some platforms have cut corners on medical review to speed up enrollment, Sprout Health maintains the standard that every GLP-1 prescription is authorized by a licensed physician after reviewing the patient's health profile.

Their platform is nationwide, their intake is streamlined without being automated to the point of cutting clinical corners, and their compliance profile is clean — no active FDA enforcement action as of our last review. For patients who want a straightforward, medically responsible GLP-1 program without the extensive lab work and higher price point of more comprehensive programs, Sprout Health represents a solid middle ground.

Physician Oversight Model

Every prescription issued through Sprout Health is reviewed and authorized by a licensed physician — not a nurse practitioner working from a rigid checklist and not an algorithm. This distinction matters clinically. GLP-1 medications have contraindications that require physician-level assessment, including history of pancreatitis, medullary thyroid carcinoma, and certain gastrointestinal conditions.

Maintaining full physician oversight on every prescription is a commitment to patient safety that not every telehealth platform makes. Sprout Health makes it clearly.

Nationwide Availability

Sprout Health provides coverage across all 50 US states. This is a meaningful differentiator — some telehealth GLP-1 programs have licensing gaps that exclude certain states. Patients in states that have historically been underserved by telehealth platforms — including rural states and states with more restrictive telehealth regulations — may find Sprout Health accessible where others are not. Confirm your specific state directly before enrolling.
